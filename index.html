Question 1
A cargo container at Port Zante has mass 120 kg. A crane lifts it with a cable. Find the tension in the cable when:
  • (a) the container is at rest
  • (b) it accelerates upward at 2 m/s¬≤
  • (c) it accelerates downward at 1.5 m/s¬≤

üí° Hint

Choose upward as positive direction.

Forces: Tension T (up), Weight mg (down)

(a) Equilibrium: T = mg

(b) Net upward: T - mg = ma

(c) Net downward: T - mg = ma (where a is negative)

Use g = 10 m/s¬≤

‚úì Answer

(a) T = 1200 N
(b) T = 1440 N
(c) T = 1020 N

üìù Step-by-Step Solution

Given: m = 120 kg, g = 10 m/s¬≤
(a) At rest (a = 0):
T - mg = 0
T = 120 √ó 10 = 1200 N
(b) Accelerating upward (a = 2 m/s¬≤):
T - mg = ma
T = mg + ma = m(g + a)
T = 120(10 + 2) = 120 √ó 12 = 1440 N
(c) Accelerating downward (a = -1.5 m/s¬≤):
T - mg = ma
T = m(g + a) = 120(10 - 1.5)
T = 120 √ó 8.5 = 1020 N
Tension increases when accelerating up, decreases when accelerating down
